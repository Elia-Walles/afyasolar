# AfyaSolar Energy Management Platform - Demo Access Guide

## Overview

AfyaSolar is a comprehensive energy management platform for healthcare facilities in Tanzania, featuring:

- **Facility Management**: Solar systems for health centers, dispensaries, clinics, and pharmacies
- **Microgrid System**: Staff housing and nearby business connections powered by facility solar systems
- **Payment Models**: Upfront, Installment, and Energy-as-a-Service (EaS)
- **Smart Metering**: Only applied to microgrid consumers, not main facilities
- **Carbon Calculator**: Automatic CO2 avoidance tracking
- **Tariff Management**: Facility-specific pricing for optimal revenue

## Demo routes in this repo

This repo implements the design token demo routes under:

- `/demo/[token]`
- `/demo/facility/[token]`
- `/demo/microgrid/[token]`

Demo tokens and facility seed data are defined in `src/lib/facility-data.ts`.
