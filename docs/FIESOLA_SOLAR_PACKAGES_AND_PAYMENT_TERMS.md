## Afya Solar (for Fiesola) — Solar Packages & Payment Terms

This document is a **customer-friendly catalogue** of Afya Solar packages and the **payment terms** available for each package.

---

## Solar package options

Afya Solar packages are offered in standard sizes (and sometimes custom sizes). The common standard packages are:

- **Essential (2 kW)**: entry-level system for smaller facilities / lighter loads.
- **Plus (4.2 kW)**: mid-size system for moderate daily load and longer backup.
- **Pro (6 kW)**: higher-capacity package for larger clinics / heavier equipment use.
- **Ultra (10 kW)**: premium package for high-demand facilities and stronger resilience.
- **15 kW, 20 kW, 30 kW**: high-capacity systems for facilities with large loads or multiple critical departments.

> If a facility needs more than the standard options, a **custom-sized** package can be offered based on a site assessment.

## What each package normally includes

Each package is sold as a complete solution. Depending on the specific package configuration, it may include:

- **Solar PV modules (panels)**
- **Inverter (hybrid/solar inverter)**
- **Battery storage**
- **Protection & safety equipment** (breakers, isolators, surge protection)
- **Mounting structure and cabling**
- **Installation and commissioning** (system testing and handover)
- **Training** for facility staff (basic operation and safety)
- **Remote monitoring** (where available)
- **Warranty & support** (warranty period depends on the package specification)

Important: **the exact technical specification** (panel quantity/type, battery size, inverter model, expected output range, and backup runtime) must be taken from the final package sheet/quotation for the facility.

## Payment options (available plans)

Each package can be offered under one or more of these payment options:

### 1) Upfront cash purchase

- **How it works**: one-time payment for the full package price.
- **Best for**: facilities that prefer ownership and can pay upfront.

### 2) Installment plan

- **How it works**: an upfront deposit, then equal monthly payments over an agreed number of months.
- **Typical structure**:
  - Upfront deposit is usually **40%** of the package cash price (unless a different percentage is agreed).
  - The remaining balance is spread across the contract duration.
- **Best for**: facilities that want ownership but prefer to spread the cost.

### 3) Power-as-a-Service (PaaS) / Energy-as-a-Service

- **How it works**: a monthly service fee instead of buying the system outright.
- **Best for**: facilities that want minimal upfront cost and prefer a service model.
- **Minimum term**: typically **6 years (72 months)** unless otherwise agreed.

## What is included in the price (inclusions)

For each payment option, the quotation should clearly state whether the price includes:

- **Shipping / delivery**
- **Installation**
- **Commissioning**
- **Maintenance**

If any of the above are not included, they should be clearly listed as **excluded** and quoted separately.

---

## Payment conditions (simple, client-facing)

### Upfront cash purchase

- Full payment is made once.
- Installation scheduling and service activation follow the agreed process after payment confirmation.

### Installment plan

- **Deposit first**, then monthly payments.
- The payment schedule shows due dates and amounts.
- If there is a small rounding difference in calculations, it is normally adjusted in the **final** installment.

### PaaS / EAAS

- Monthly fee is billed according to the agreed billing model (fixed monthly or metered, if applicable).
- Minimum commitment period applies (typically 72 months).
